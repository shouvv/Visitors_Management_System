Visitor Information

Self Declaration - Medical

WRITTEN BY Pardeep Kumar - Security Specialist - Pantnagar
VERIFIED BY Manvendra Singh - Plant HR Head - Pantnagar
APPROVED BY Ravindra Singh Rawat - Plant Head - Pantnagar

ENTRY TO PRODUCTION AREAS IS SUBJECT TO THE VISITOR/CONTRACTOR COMPLYING WITH THE FOLLOWING RULES.

  • Hair properly covered with hair net in production area
  • Wear company issued personnel protective/safety gauges like gloves, apron, and safety glasses
  • Wear beard cover if have in production area
  • Use antibacterial hand cleaner and hand wash basin at appropriate points
  • Remove all jewellery and watches except plain ring and sleeper earrings
  • Smoking not allowed within the premises
  • No drinking within the premises
  • No drinking or eating (including chewing gum), have food in canteen
  • No nail varnish or false nails
  • During illness or injury avoid direct contact with food products
  • All cuts to be covered with a suitable bandage
  • Do not enter restricted areas unless and until instructed
  • Washing of hands before and after visit in production area
  • Entrance into the production area from the employee entrance only
  • Do follow the instruction written on signage

The information I have given is correct and I have read and understand the above rules.
