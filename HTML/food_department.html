Self Declaration - Medical

WRITTEN BY Pardeep Kumar - Security Specialist - Pantnagar
VERIFIED BY Manvendra Singh - Plant HR Head - Pantnagar
APPROVED BY Ravindra Singh Rawat - Plant Head - Pantnagar

ENTRY TO PRODUCTION AREAS IS SUBJECT TO THE VISITOR/CONTRACTOR COMPLYING WITH THE FOLLOWING RULES:

  1. Hair properly covered with hair net in production area
  2. Wear company issued personnel protective/safety gauges like gloves, apron, and safety glasses
  3. Wear beard cover if have in production area
  4. Use antibacterial hand cleaner and hand wash basin at appropriate points
  5. Remove all jewellery and watches except plain ring and sleeper earrings
  6. Smoking not allowed within the premises
  7. No drinking within the premises
  8. No drinking or eating (including chewing gum), have food in canteen
  9. No nail varnish or false nails
  10. During illness or injury avoid direct contact with food products
  11. All cuts to be covered with a suitable bandage
  12. Do not enter restricted areas unless and until instructed
  13. Washing of hands before and after visit in production area
  14. Entrance into the production area from the employee entrance only
  15. Do follow the instruction written on signage

The information I have given is correct and I have read and understand the above rules.
